Demurrage = penalty when vessel waits too long.

Factors:
- Predicted loading hours
- Weather-related downtime
- ROM supply delays
- Jetty congestion

Risk Levels:
- HIGH → cost > $50,000
- MEDIUM → $20,000–50,000
- LOW → < $20,000

Shipping Planner must proactively:
- Reassign vessels
- Increase hauling supply
- Adjust loading slots
