Flow:
PIT → ROM → Hauling → Jetty → Vessel Loading

Mining → Hauling → Shipping must stay balanced.

Rules:
1. Production < shipping demand → deficit → demurrage risk.
2. Production > shipping → stockpile overflow.
3. High-risk days require rescheduling.
4. Manager focuses on:
   - Profit
   - Cost (mining+hauling+demurrage)
   - Efficiency and delays
   - Weather disruptions
   - Resource allocation

Executives expect:
- concise daily summary
- bottleneck identification
- risk scoring
- recommendations for optimization
